# Poisson Model
___
Class: [[Probability & Statistics]]
Type: Lecture
Tags: # 
Date: October 14th, 2025
___

This model is a generalisation of the [[Binomial Model]], in the sense that it allows *the probability of success to vary* at each trial.

The number $P(n;k)$ is the coefficient of $x^k$ in the polynomial expansion $(p_1x+q_1)...(p_nx+q_n)$
